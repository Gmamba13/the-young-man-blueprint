1. What We Collect

When you create an account: your name, email address, and password (stored locally in your browser).

  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers

Emails are sent through Web3Forms and Brevo, both of which maintain industry-standard security practices.

Payments are processed by Stripe, which is PCI DSS compliant. We never handle or store payment card information directly.
