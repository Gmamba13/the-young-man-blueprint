THE PURPOSE FINDER
Answer these three questions. We'll generate a purpose statement you can come back to when motivation fades.
1. What makes you angry when you see it in the world?
Your anger often points to what you deeply care about.
2. What comes naturally to you that others find difficult?
Your natural strengths are clues to your contribution.
3. What would you do every day even if no one paid you?
Intrinsic motivation is your most durable fuel.
