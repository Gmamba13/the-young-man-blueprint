📞CRISIS & SUPPORT LINES6 RESOURCES

If you're in crisis or struggling beyond what self-help can reach — these resources exist specifically for that. Please use them. There is no strength in suffering alone when trained support is available.

988 SUICIDE & CRISIS LIFELINE
Call or Text: 988

US and Canada. Free, confidential, available 24/7. For any mental health crisis — not just suicidal ideation.
SAMARITANS (UK)
Call: 116 123

Free, confidential, 24/7. For anyone struggling emotionally or in crisis in the UK and Ireland.
LIFELINE AUSTRALIA
Call: 13 11 14

24/7 crisis support for Australians. Also available via chat at lifeline.org.au.
SAMHSA ADDICTION HELPLINE
Call: 1-800-662-4357

Free, confidential treatment referrals for substance use disorders. Available 24/7 in the US.
FIND A THERAPIST
psychologytoday.com

Filter by location, cost, specialty, and insurance. The best directory for finding professional mental health support.
EATING DISORDER HELPLINE
Call: 1-866-662-1235

The National Alliance for Eating Disorders helpline. For men struggling with disordered eating.
