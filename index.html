The Path

YOUR 10-STEP BLUEPRINT

Ten steps that move you from where you are now to where you need to be. Each step unlocks based on your XP progress — personalized to you.

Step 01DAY 1
TAKE STOCK

Honestly assess where you are. Sleep, finances, mental state, relationships. No judgement — just truth. You can't fix what you won't look at.

🧠 Mental audit💵 Net worth snapshot📋 Pillar self-assessment
Step 02WEEK 1
STABILIZE YOUR BODY

Sleep 7–9 hours. Drink water. Move daily. These three things alone will change your energy, mood, and mental clarity within a week.

🛌 Sleep schedule set💧 2L water daily🚶 Daily movement
Step 03WEEK 2
STOP THE BLEEDING

Identify the one habit, behaviour, or pattern doing the most damage right now. Stop it. Whether it's spending, porn, substances, or avoidance — this is step three.

🚫 Worst habit identified📵 First 7-day clean🔒 Triggers mapped
Step 04WEEK 3–4
BUILD YOUR MONEY FLOOR

Get a budget. Know what's coming in and going out. Save your first $500 emergency fund. Credit score checked. This is your financial foundation.

💰 Budget created🏦 $500 saved📊 Credit checked
Step 05MONTH 2
BUILD YOUR PHYSICAL FOUNDATION

Start a training program you can maintain. 3 days a week is enough. Nail your protein target. Your body is the hardware — everything else runs on it.

💪 Training program started🥩 Protein target hit⚡ 2-week workout streak
Step 06MONTH 2–3
GET YOUR MIND RIGHT

Journal daily. Understand your anxiety, anger, or emptiness — don't just suppress it. If therapy is needed, book it. This is strength, not weakness.

✍️ Daily journaling🧠 Therapy/counselling explored🔥 30-day mission streak
Step 07MONTH 3–4
LOCK IN YOUR CAREER PATH

Know what you're building toward. Pick the skill. Update the resume. Have one real career conversation. Salary negotiation starts now.

🎯 Career direction chosen📄 Resume updated🤝 One career conversation
Step 08MONTH 4–5
START BUILDING WEALTH

Open a TFSA or Roth IRA. Buy your first index fund. Even $50. Automate it. The money habit is more important than the amount at this stage.

📈 Investment account opened💸 First investment made🔄 Auto-invest set up
Step 09MONTH 5–8
STRENGTHEN YOUR RELATIONSHIPS

Reach out to men you've lost touch with. Be honest in your close relationships. Cut the ones draining you. Build the kind of connections that actually sustain you.

🤝 3 real friendships invested in✂️ Toxic ties addressed💬 Honest conversations had
Step 10MONTH 9–12
BLUEPRINT COMPLETE — NOW LEAD

You've built all 11 pillars. You're not the same man. Now your job is to maintain, optimize, and help the next man. The real work never ends — it just changes.

🏆 All pillars active💰 6-month runway saved📚 Mentoring another man⭐ Blueprint Elite rank
